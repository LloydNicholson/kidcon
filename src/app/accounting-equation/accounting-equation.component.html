ADELIC

Test your ADELIC knowledge by using the following activities below. These activities test your understanding of the ADELIC principle.

Account Classification

There are various different types of classifications such as Assets, Liabilities etc. and the only way to know them is to learn them parrot style. Repeat, Repeat, Repeat!

ADELIC Principle Classification

Using ADELIC is a skill, guideline or map used to find the correct side that an account increases or decreases. Make sure you know your account names and each of their classifications and accounting becomes easy. Identifying which side each of these different accounts increase on will make your general ledger, trial balance and accounting equation transactions easier.
