ADELIC

Test your ADELIC knowledge by using the following activities below. These activities test your understanding of the ADELIC principle. ADELIC is an acronym for Assets, Drawings, Expenses, Liabilities, Incomes and Capital

Account Classification

There are various different types of classifications such as Assets, Liabilities etc. and the only way to know them is to learn them parrot style. Repeat, Repeat, Repeat!

ADELIC Principle Classification

Using ADELIC is a skill. It should be used as guideline or map used to find the correct side that an account increases or decreases. Make sure you know your account names and each of their classifications. Once you know each of these, the ADELIC principle becomes easy to use. Identifying which side each of these different accounts increase on will make your general ledger, trial balance and accounting equation transactions easier.

Identifying your debits and credits can help reduce stress when you are writing an accounting test or exam. It helps you to feel more confident. The more you practice, the better you will get and the preparation done here will prepare your for the accounting equations and general ledgers you will do in the future.
